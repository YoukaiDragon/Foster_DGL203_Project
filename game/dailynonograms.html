Daily Nonograms

Click on a tile to cycle it between blank / filled / crossed out. The numbers on the edge of each row/column show how many tiles should be filled in for that row/column. Each number is the length of a consecutive group of filled tiles, with multiple numbers representing that many groups each separated by at least one tile
