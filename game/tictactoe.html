How to play

Goal: Be the first player to mark three tiles in a row

  • Click on a cell to mark it with the turn player's symbol
  • Players alternate marking a cell
  • The first player to have three symbols in a row wins
  • If all cells are filled and there is no winner, the game ends in a draw
